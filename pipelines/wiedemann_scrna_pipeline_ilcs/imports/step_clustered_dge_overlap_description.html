Calculates the overlap of differentially expressed genes between clusters.

Output
For each clustering group a overlapping_dge.csv is produced containing the following columns:

  • Gene: the respective gene name
  • Regulation: the respective regulation of the gene (up or down)
  • Overlap: in how many clusters the gene showed the respective regulation
  • Clusters: a semi-colon separated list of the clusters the gene has been identified as differentially expressed with the respective regulation
